Tribute to Pelé

Edson Arantes do Nascimento

October 23, 1940 - December 29, 2022

Pelé was a Brazilian professional footballer who played as a forward. Widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, he was among the most successful and popular sports figures of the 20th century.

Achievements

  • 3 FIFA World Cup titles (1958, 1962, 1970)
  • 1,283 career goals in 1,363 games
  • Named FIFA Player of the Century in 1999

Legacy

Pelé's influence on football is unparalleled, with a career that inspired millions and helped popularize the sport around the world.
